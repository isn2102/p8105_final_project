Economic Investment

Column

Gardens by Budget/Funding Allocation

Visualization of the distribution of community gardens in New York City, colored by participatory budget allocated to each community board. NA values reflect community boards that do not engage in participatory budgeting. Budget allocation: total amount of participatory funding for projects in that community board. Participatory budget: process where community members vote of projects that they want funding to be allocated towards.

Market Value

Visualization of the distribution of community gardens in New York City, colored by average revised market value in each community board, which is updated twice a year (most recently, Feb 2020). Average revised market value: updated original market value.

Column

Poverty

Visualization of the distribution of community gardens in New York City, colored by average percent poverty in each community board location. Poverty: percentage of residents living below 100% of NYCs calculated poverty threshold, based on income and necessary expenses.

Rent Burden

Visualization of the distribution of community gardens in New York City, colored by average percent of monthly income spent on rent in each community board location. Rent burden: percentage of renter-occupied homes whose gross rent is greater than or equal to 30% of the household income in the past 12 months.
